Pig-Dice!!

Welcome to pig-dice

Here are the rules!

  • Both players input their username
  • Player 1 proceeds to play first by pressing the 'roll' button
  • Player 1 then presses the 'hold' button to allow player 2 to play
  • Player 2 proceeds to play second by pressing the 'roll' button
  • Player 2 then presses the 'hold' button to allow player 1 to play
  • The cycle continues untill the first player who gets to 100 first, is declared the winner!
